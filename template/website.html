About

Why sustainable?

By buying from eco friendly companies, we are helping out the environment and paving the road to a greener future. It also reduces the carbon footprints we carry on the ecosystem and maintain habitats for important animals in our life.

Is the price worth it?

These products, though on the expensive end, are well worth the price! They are more durable and are for a good cause.

Our Goals

We are a team of high school sophomores from the Bay Area devoted to preserving the environment. We strive to make a larger impact on the world, and aim to maintain the worldâ€™s natural resources and keep them lasting for future generations, which can only be possible by being more conscious about the environment and how we as a society treat it. Through this website we want to deliver enviromentally conscious alternatives to people anywhere, anytime to help the environment.

What we did

We decided that the best way to save the environment is to do it together. That is why we decided to create a project that anyone in the can use. To inspire more people to join this movement, we decided to focus our project starting one small step at a time. The items in our search bar are the daily items that everyone uses without realizing that there are eco-friendly alternatives. In order to make our idea more accessible to people, we decided to make it a website such that they can easily access it on any device, whether its a computer, phone, or tablet. We give people access to a directory of environment friendly alternatives to common items such that they can incorporate these into their daily activities.
